Watch the video to learn how Trellis is changing research

Trellis is a suite of software tools for creating, administering, and collecting survey data. What separates Trellis from other data collection tools is its support for mapping social networks.

After enumerating all respondents in the sample, the surveyors can ask questions such as "Who do you consider your best friend?" or "Who do you go to for health advice?" A respondent can identify the named social contact by photograph.

This software can be downloaded free of charge
